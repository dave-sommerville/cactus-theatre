Cactus Theatre Co was created in memory of our friend Daniel Gilmour, who left the world’s stage too soon. He wrote Cactus in November 2023.  In the play, one character informs us, “In many First Nations cultures, cacti are symbols of endurance and love.” The cactus has now become our symbol of enduring love for Daniel. See you on the flip side.
