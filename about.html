Cactus Theatre Co. was founded in memory of our friend and collaborator, Daniel Gilmour — a luminous soul who left the world's stage far too soon. In his final piece, Cactus, Daniel wrote:

“In many First Nations cultures, cacti are symbols of endurance and love.”

These words now serve as both a guiding light and a quiet echo of Daniel's spirit. The cactus, resilient and full of quiet strength, has become our emblem — not only of our enduring love for Daniel, but of the passion, generosity, and creativity he brought to every rehearsal, every line, every moment. Through Cactus Theatre Co., we carry forward Daniel's voice, his questions, his laughter — continuing to share the stage with him in the only way we can now: through story, through community, through the art he so deeply believed in.
